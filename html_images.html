Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kelantan Branch has two main campuses: the permanent campus in Bukit Ilmu, Machang, which has been in operation since 1996, and a city campus in Lembah Sireh, Kota Bharu, established in 2007. Both campuses offer various academic programs for students in Kelantan, with the Machang campus providing extensive residential facilities that can accommodate over 5,000 students. UiTM Kelantan serves as an important center for higher education in the region, equipping students with the necessary knowledge and skills.
